Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 June 2026

RateFinder is an informational app. It does not provide financial advice or process money exchange transactions.

Information We Collect

RateFinder does not require users to create an account. We do not ask for your name, identity document, bank account, card details, or transaction information.

  • Location: If you allow location access, the app may use your approximate or precise location to show nearby money changers and distance estimates.
  • Favorites: Saved money changers are stored locally on your device.
  • App usage and diagnostics: We may collect basic app events, crash logs, and performance data to improve reliability.
  • Advertising: If ads are enabled, Google AdMob may collect data according to Google’s advertising and privacy policies.

How We Use Information

  • To show nearby exchange rate listings.
  • To save your preferences and favorites.
  • To improve app performance and fix issues.
  • To display advertisements if monetization is enabled.

Location Data

Location access is optional. If you deny location permission, RateFinder may show popular Kuala Lumpur or Malaysia listings instead. We do not sell your location data.

Third-Party Services

RateFinder may use public rate sources and third-party services such as Google AdMob, Apple frameworks, and analytics or crash reporting tools. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Exchange Rate Data

Rates are collected from supported public sources and may change at any time. RateFinder does not guarantee that displayed rates are final or available at the counter.

Children’s Privacy

RateFinder is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.
