Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a rate unavailable?

Some money changers may not publish rates for every currency. Rate availability depends on the source and selected currency.

What does Buy Rate mean?

Buy Rate is from the money changer’s perspective. It is the rate used when the money changer buys foreign currency from you.

What does Sell Rate mean?

Sell Rate is from the money changer’s perspective. It is the rate used when the money changer sells foreign currency to you.

Do I need an account?

No. RateFinder does not require login for the current app experience.

Which currencies are supported?

RateFinder currently focuses on USD, SGD, EUR, GBP, AUD, JPY, THB, and CNY.

Are rates guaranteed?

No. Rates may change before you reach the shop. Always confirm the final rate at the counter.
